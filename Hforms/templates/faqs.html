FAQs

1) Does this service take personal details of the user?
A) That's an astronomical no, we do not take any details from you, not even your email address.

2) Do I need to create an account or have to login to fill a form?
A) No, you do not need to login or create an account to fill out a form.

3) Can I recover my password if I forget it by any chance?
A) We do not take your email address so there is no way we would be able to send you any reset link to change password.

4) How far is my data encrypted?
A) Your data is encrypted only on our servers, once you download the csv file it's decrypted on your end.

5) What if there are bugs that I'd like to report?
A) You can open an issue on our github repository so that we can fix the issue as soon as possible.

6) Are there any guidelines on the number of questions?
A) You have to make a minimum of 2 questions.
